August was a costly month, -24.92PTS, not something anyone would want. However, just after the halfway mark in August, we brought in 8.2PTS profit, and there was hope we could have a month of profit. Towards the end of the month, it was loss after loss. The only hope was the last Saturday of the month, which still, was not enough. We look forward to September where we hope to start and end strong!
